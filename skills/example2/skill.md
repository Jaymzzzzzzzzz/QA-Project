## Percent Change
((new_value - old_value) / old_value) * 100

## Difference
new_value - old_value

## Ratio / Share
(part / total) * 100

## Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR)
((end / start) ** (1 / years)) - 1) * 100

## Average
sum(values) / count(values)

## Units Warning
- Always note whether values are in thousands, millions, or billions
- Do NOT convert units unless the question specifically asks
- Return the number exactly as it appears in the document
